Pesto Pasta

A refreshing pasta dish

Quick and easy, ready within 20 minutes and with plenty of vegetables!

Ingredients

  • Pesto sauce
  • 400g pasta
  • 400g chicken
  • Couple handfuls of chopped cherry tomatoes
  • Half bag of spinach
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with boiling water and add the pasta
  2. Cook the chicken separately
  3. Chop red onion and add olive oil and onion to pan, cook until onion has softened
  4. Add pesto sauce and chopped tomatoes
  5. Add spinach once tomatoes have wilted
  6. Once spinach has wilted, add cooked pasta and combine together
  7. Serve, with chicken added on top
